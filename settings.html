Settings

How to configure RuneCord

Command Ignoring

Ignore specific commands

Usage: )settings ignored (scopes) (commands)

Scopes: Either a @User, #channel, or server.
Commands: Either a ~command, ~all of a prefix, or all commands.

Scopes and commands are separated with a |.
Example: )settings ignored (@User 1 | @User 2 | #no-bot) ()all | ~ping)

Unignoring wworks the same way, just use unignore instead.

If there is only one scope, you can omit the parenthesis.

Checking Settings

Check the current settings for RuneCord.

Usage: )settings ignored <server|@User|#channel>
Example: )settings ignored #channel-name
