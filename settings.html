Configuring RuneCord for your Server

Command Ignoring

)settings ignore (scopes) (commands)

Scopes: Either a @User, #channel, or the server.
Commands: Either a ~command, ~all of a prefix, or all commands.

Scopes and commands are separated with a |.
Example: )settings ignore (@User | @User 2 | #no-bot) ()all | ~ping)

Unignoring works the same way. Just use unignore instead.

If there is only one scope, you can omit the parenthesis.

Checking settings

)settings ignored <server|@user|#channel>
