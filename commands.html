Normal Commands

Commands that can be used by everyone

Command Description Cooldown Aliases
About Shows you some information about RuneCord. Also contains some useful links.
Usage: ~about		 5 seconds info
Adventurer's Log Gets a small table of the last 10 entries of user's adventurer's log. Username required.
Usage: ~alog <username>		 10 seconds advlog, log
Araxxi Rotation Tells you the current rotation for Araxxor/Araxxi. Also tells you when the next path closes.
Usage: ~araxxi		 5 seconds rax, spooder
Big Chinchompa Tells you when the next Big Chinchompa D&D will begin.
Usage: ~bigchin		 5 seconds  
Guthixian Cache Tells you when the next Guthixian Cache D&D will begin.
Usage: ~cache		 5 seconds  
Circus Location Gives you the curent location of the circus.
Usage: ~circus		 5 seconds  
Troll Invation Tells you the amount of experience you'd gain if you completed a full round of Troll Invasion. Level required.
Usage: ~invasion <level>		 5 seconds troll, trollinvasion
Invite Get the link to add the bot to another server.
Usage: ~invite		 5 seconds join, oauth
Jack of Trades Tells you the amount of experience you'd gain if you completed your Jack of Trades aura. Tier & level required.
Usage: ~jot <normal|master|supreme|legendary> <level>		 5 seconds  
Experience Lamps Tells you how much experience you'd get from a lamp. Size & level required.
Usage: ~lamp <small|med|large|huge> <level>		 5 seconds xplamp
Penguins Tells you how much experience or gold you'd get from penguins. Points required, level optional.
Usage: ~pengs <points> [level]		 5 seconds  
Ping Used to check if the bot is working.
Usage: ~ping		 5 seconds p
Item Prices Returns the price for an item. Item name required.
Usage: ~price <item name>		 10 seconds  
Prifddinas Raven Informs you if there is a raven in Prifddinas or nor.
Usage: ~raven		 5 seconds  
Reset Time Used to get how long until the game resets.
Usage: ~reset		 5 seconds  
Roll / Random Number Roll a number between the given range. Min & Max required.
Usage: ~roll <min> <max>		 5 seconds random
Rise of the Six Rotation Tells you what brothers are what side.
Usage: ~rots		 5 seconds  
Sinkholes Tells you how long until the next Sinkhole D&D spawns.
Usage: ~sinkhole		 5 seconds  
Spotlight Tells you what minigame is currently on spotlight.
Usage: ~spotlight		 5 seconds  
User Stats Gives you a nice table with a user's statistics. Username required.
Usage: ~stats <username>		 10 seconds hs
God Statues Gives you how much experience you'd gain for completing God Statues.
Usage: ~statues <username>		 10 seconds  
Vis wax Combination Gets the current Vis wax combination from the RuneScape Official Forums.
Usage: ~viswax		 10 seconds  
Vorago Rotation Tells you what rotation Vorago is currently on.
Usage: ~vorago		 5 seconds rago
Voice of Seren Tells you what the current Voice of Seren is.
Usage: ~vos		 10 seconds voice
Warbands Tells you when the next Warbands D&D will begin.
Usage: ~warbands		 5 seconds wbs

Command Description Cooldown Aliases
Settings Adjust the settings for how the bot works on the server.
Usage: See Settings.		 3 seconds set, config
Bot Statistics Get a wide range of statistics about the bot.
Usage: )stats		 10 seconds
