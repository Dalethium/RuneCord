Command List

  • Normal

Key: [optional] <required>

About

Shows you some info about RuneCord. Also contains some useful links.
~about
Aliases: info
Cooldown: 5 seconds

Adenturer's Log

Gets a small table of the last 5 entries of a user's adventurer's log.
~alog <username>
Aliases: advlog, log
Cooldown: 10 seconds

Araxxi Rotation

Tells you what path is currently closed for Araxxor/Araxxi. Also tells you what the next path to be closed will be and how many days until then.
~araxxi
Aliases: rax, spooder
Cooldown: 5 seconds

Big Chinchompa

Tells you when the next Big Chinchompa D&D will begin.
~bigchin
Cooldown: 5 seconds

Guthixan Cache

Tells you when the next Guthixan Cache D&D will begin.
~cache
Cooldown: 5 seconds

Circus Location

Gives you the current location of the circus. Also gives you the next location and many days until it switches.
~circus
Cooldown: 5 seconds

Troll Invasion

Tells you the amount of experience you would gain if you completed the Troll Invasion D&D in its entirety.
~invasion <level>
Aliases: troll, trollinvasion
Cooldown: 5 seconds

Invite

Get the link to add the bot to another server.
~invite
Aliases: join, oauth
Cooldown: 5 seconds

Jack of Trades

Tells you the amount of experience you'd gain if you completed your Jack of Trades aura. Accepts all tiers.
~jot <normal|master|supreme|legendary> <level>
Cooldown: 5 seconds

Experience Lamps

Tells you how much experience you'd get from a lamp. Accepts all sizes.
~lamp <small|med|large|huge> <level>
Aliases: xplamp
Cooldown: 5 seconds

Penguins

Tells you how much experience you'd gain or how much gold you'd gain based on how many points you have and your level.

If you don't enter a level, it will tell you how much gold you'd gain.
~pengs <points> [level]
Cooldown: 5 seconds

Ping

Used to check if the bot is working. Replies with various sayings and the response delay.
~ping
Aliases: p
Cooldown: 5 seconds

Item Prices

Returns the price for an item based on item name.
~price <item name>
Cooldown: 10 seconds

Prifddina Raven

Informs you if there is a raven in Prifddinas or not.
~raven
Cooldown: 5 seconds

Reset Time

Used to get the time when the game resets. Replies with how long until reset.
~reset
Cooldown: 5 seconds

Roll / Random Number

Roll a number between the given range. By default 1-10.
~roll [[min-]max]
Aliases: random
Cooldown: 5 seconds

Rise of the Six Rotation

Tells you what brothers are on what side for Rise of the Six. Also tells you where they'll be next time they switch and when they switch.
~rots
Cooldown: 5 seconds

Sinholes

Tells you how long until the next Sinkhole D&D spawns.
~sinkhole
Cooldown: 5 seconds

Minigame Spotlight

Tells you what minigame is currently on spotlight, as well as what minigame will be on spotlight next and when.
~spotlight
Cooldown: 5 seconds

User Stats

Gives you a nice table with a user's statistics.
~stats <username>
Aliases: hs
Cooldown: 10 seconds

God Statues

Gives you how much experience you'd gain for completing God Statues, based on your username.
~statues <username>
Cooldown: 10 seconds

Game Time

~time
Aliases: gametime

Viswax Combination

~viswax

~vorago
