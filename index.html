Meet the creators of RuneCord

Witty    

Creator and Owner of RuneCord

Witty, also known as unlucky4ever started RuneCord as a hobby project back in April 2016. Since then the project has grown exponentially with over 3,000 servers using it and being recognized by Discord and Jagex.
